Word Frequency Calculator and Analysis.
Make sure to download the required modules from requirements.txt to use the graphing feature.

Command to show 10 most common words throughout the passage:
python3 -m parser

Command to graph the occurrance of the most common word throughout the passage:
python3 -m graph



Fun fact:
The following pages from the imports/Mandelbrot folder are from Benoit Mandelbrot's "The Misbehavior of Markets, A Fractal View of Financial Turbulence." Mandelbrot, the father of geometry, actually wrote his doctoral thesis on the fractal power law that rules the mathematical frequency with which individual words occur in common language. The passage included in this program discusses his "aha!" moment, when he first thought of the idea to do it. 

His thesis:
